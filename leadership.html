So you wanna be a leader?

Running Animation Anteaters is a team effort

All of these positions are crucial in creating the team and making the club an awesome experience for everyone. Please do not discourage yourselves by saying "oh….I’m not fit for leading" or "I don’t know if I can pull this off." Leading a club has its difficulties but it has a lot of rewarding and fun aspects to it. You get to meet a lot of talented and lovely art peers. As long as you enjoyed being part of Animation Anteaters in the previous years, we say, go ahead and apply. You’ve seen Animation Anteaters and you probably have seen aspects of the club that have been fun or aspects that should be improved. With that knowledge, we hope that you can improve Animation Anteaters and make it an even greater experience for the new members. We have four positions you can apply for:

Director

Executive Director
  • Schedules officer meetings
  • Leads officer meetings and club meetings
  • Logistic stuff (ie making sure club is an actual club, reserving rooms etc)
  • Make sure people are making deadlines
  • Remind people to do their stuff if they're falling behind
  • When2Meet for meetings
  • Take notes during officer meeting
  • Can pick up Director's responsibilities if he/she is getting busy/needs help

Creative Producer

Publicity
  • Leads and organizes creative parts of meetings
  • Helps plan social events
  • Updates website
  • Updates Facebook
  • Sends emails
  • Creates surveys
  • Helps plan social events

All Officer's Responsibilities
  • Weekly Officer Meetings
  • Brainstorming/discussing ideas
  • Coming up with quarter and meeting plans
  • Helping create examples for the projects
  • Going to the offical club meetings


    • Please expect to and be willing to put in at least 2-7 hours per week.
